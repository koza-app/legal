Privacy Policy

AI-Powered Baby Tracking and Mother's Health
Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Koza application ("the App"), developed and published by Kılınç Labs, collects, processes, stores, and protects your personal data.

Continued use of the App indicates that you have read and accepted this policy. If you do not accept this policy, please do not use the App.

Koza is an AI-powered baby tracking and mother's health application intended for parents aged 18 and over. The App provides baby feeding, sleep, diaper, growth, vaccination, and milestone tracking; mother's health tracking (mood, weight, postpartum recovery, postnatal depression screening); and AI-powered daily insights, weekly reports, and a question-and-answer assistant.

Important Medical Notice: Koza is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. All AI outputs, postpartum depression screening results, growth charts, vaccination reminders, and health content in the App are for informational purposes only. For any concerns regarding your baby's or your own health, please consult a qualified healthcare professional. In emergencies, call 112 (Turkey) or your local emergency number.

2. Data Controller

  • Data Controller: Kılınç Labs
  • Application Name: Koza
  • Contact Email: koza.ai@proton.me

3. Personal Data Collected

3.1 Data Collected During Registration

  • Email address (obtained only through Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google; Koza does not require a separate email/password registration)
  • Name or nickname (entered by the user during onboarding)

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Push notification token (only if notification permission is granted)
  • Supabase Auth JWT token for authentication session
  • Application error and crash reports (via Sentry, without personal data)

Location data, contact lists, photo library, and device identifiers are strictly not collected.

3.3 Baby Data (Entered by the User)

The following baby data is manually entered into the App by the parent or legal caregiver:

  • Baby's name, date of birth, gender, profile photo (optional)
  • Feeding records (breastfeeding duration, side, bottle amount, solid food, pumping)
  • Sleep records (start/end time, sleep quality)
  • Diaper change records (type, time)
  • Growth measurements (height, weight, head circumference)
  • Vaccination schedule records (vaccines administered, side effects, notes)
  • Developmental milestones (motor, language, social, cognitive categories)
  • Illness records (type, fever, symptoms, severity)
  • Medication records (name, dosage, time)
  • Solid food and allergy records

3.4 Mother's Health Data (Entered by the User)

  • Mother profile (date of birth, delivery date, delivery type — vaginal/cesarean, feeding method)
  • Mood records (daily mood, energy level, sleep quality, tags)
  • Weight measurements
  • Postpartum/recovery records (vaginal healing, cesarean wound, hair loss, breast health, pelvic floor, emotional state, etc.)
  • Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (EPDS) test responses and scores

3.5 AI Assistant Interaction Data

  • Questions sent to the AI assistant and received answers (chat history)
  • Daily insight, weekly report, and pediatric report texts
  • AI-generated nutrition and activity suggestions

3.6 Sensitive Personal Data Classification

Baby and mother's health data (including feeding, sleep, growth, vaccinations, illnesses, mood, depression screening) is classified as "sensitive personal data" under Article 6 of the Turkish Data Protection Law (KVKK) and as "special categories of personal data" under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This data is processed only with the user's explicit consent, obtained via the AI Usage Consent screen during onboarding, and can be withdrawn at any time (see Section 9).

4. Purposes of Data Processing

The collected data is processed solely for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Operation of baby and mother's health tracking functions
  • Personalization: AI generating custom insights based on baby's age, mother's postpartum period, and existing records
  • Analytics: Growth percentiles, sleep trends, feeding patterns, and weekly report calculations
  • Notifications: Vaccination reminders, milestone expectations, mood check reminders, and weekly report notifications
  • Security: Account verification, prevention of unauthorized access, and data integrity

Your data is not used for advertising purposes, not shared with third-party advertising networks, not sold, and not shared for marketing purposes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Your personal data is processed under the following legal bases of the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK):

  • KVKK Article 5/2-c — Performance of a Contract: Data processed for the purposes of registration, authentication, and provision of app services.
  • KVKK Article 5/2-f — Legitimate Interest: Data processed for the purposes of improving service quality and maintaining security infrastructure.
  • KVKK Article 6/3 — Explicit Consent: Consent required for processing of sensitive personal data (health data) and sharing with AI services. This consent is obtained during onboarding and can be withdrawn at any time.
  • KVKK Article 5/1 — Explicit Consent: Consent required for push notifications. Can be revoked at any time via app settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers to deliver the app's services. These providers can only access data as necessary for the service and are subject to their own privacy policies.

All third-party providers listed below — especially the AI service provider Google — are contractually obligated to provide protection equal to or greater than that defined in this Privacy Policy. Kılınç Labs only works with providers meeting these security standards.

6.1 Supabase

Infrastructure and data storage provider. All user data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. Row Level Security (RLS) ensures each user can only access their own data.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

6.2 Google Gemini (AI Service)

AI service provider used for daily insights, weekly reports, the Q&A assistant, and nutrition suggestions. Koza accesses the Google Gemini API through a paid Tier 1 plan. Under this plan, transmitted data is not used by Google for model training and is not subject to human review.

The following data is sent to the Gemini service:

  • Baby age and current record summaries (breastfeeding, sleep, diaper, growth statistics)
  • Mother's postpartum period information (days since delivery, delivery type, feeding method)
  • Questions sent to the AI assistant
  • Milestone records (for developmental evaluation)
  • EPDS test responses (for supportive feedback generation)

Your email address, payment information, or account credentials are strictly never transmitted to Gemini. Transmitted data is used solely to process your request.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6.3 RevenueCat

Subscription and purchase management provider. User identifiers and App Store / Google Play purchase information are processed.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

6.4 Apple and Google (Authentication)

OAuth providers used for registration and sign-in. Each provider's own privacy standards apply.

  • Apple: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy

6.5 Expo / EAS

Application distribution infrastructure provider.

  • Privacy policy: expo.dev/privacy

6.6 Sentry

Error and crash reporting provider. Only anonymous error metadata is transmitted; personal data or health data is strictly not shared.

  • Privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy

7. International Data Transfer

Supabase, Google Gemini, RevenueCat, and Sentry servers are located in the United States. Your data is therefore transferred abroad. This transfer is carried out under Article 9 of the KVKK, and the necessary technical and administrative measures are taken.

8. Data Security

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) are stored encrypted on your device.
  • The app does not use web cookies.
  • Premium membership fields can only be modified by the server; they are closed to direct client-side access.
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) ensures each user can only access their own data.
  • Every call to the AI service is made after session authentication of the user.
  • EPDS depression screening responses are stored in encrypted JSONB format.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

When the "Delete Account" action is performed, all the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Profile information (mother and baby)
  • All feeding, sleep, diaper, and growth records
  • Vaccination, milestone, illness, medication records
  • Solid food and allergy records
  • Mother's mood, weight, postpartum records
  • EPDS depression screening responses and results
  • AI chat history (Q&A conversations)
  • Daily insight and weekly report records
  • Smart reminder records

9.1 Withdrawing AI Consent

On first use, you are asked to give explicit consent for sharing your data with the Google Gemini AI service (AI Usage Consent screen). The app's core AI features (daily insight, weekly report, Q&A assistant, milestone evaluation) depend on this consent.

To withdraw consent:

  • From within the app: Open the app → follow Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all your data, immediately stopping AI sharing.
  • By email: If you cannot access the app, contact koza.ai@proton.me to request account deletion. The request will be processed within 30 days.

After the account is deleted, data previously transmitted to Google Gemini is deleted according to Google's own retention policy (on the paid Tier 1 plan, data is not used for model training).

10. Notifications and Permissions

10.1 Push Notifications

If notification permission is granted, the following notification types become active; each can be turned on or off individually from the app settings:

  • Vaccination schedule reminders
  • Milestone expectation reminders
  • Weekly report notification
  • Daily mood check reminder
  • Custom reminders (medication, doctor's appointment, etc.)

Until permission is granted, no push token is recorded and no notifications are sent.

11. Child Data and Age Limit

Koza is intended only for parents or legal caregivers aged 18 and over. The app uses baby and young child data for tracking purposes; however, this data is entered not by the child but by the legal parent or caregiver.

The app does not collect personal data directly from individuals under the age of 18 under COPPA and KVKK. Users acknowledge, when creating a registration, that they are over 18 years of age and that the baby data they enter is within their parental/caregiver authority.

If you notice that a child has accidentally registered, please contact koza.ai@proton.me; the account will be deleted immediately.

12. User Rights (KVKK Article 11)

Under the Personal Data Protection Law, you have the following rights:

  • To learn whether your personal data is processed
  • To request information about processing if processed
  • To learn the purpose of processing and whether the data is used for that purpose
  • To learn the third parties to whom the data is transferred domestically or abroad
  • To request correction of incomplete or incorrectly processed data
  • To request deletion or destruction of the data under Article 7 of the KVKK
  • To object to processing
  • To request compensation for damages arising from unlawful processing

You can send your requests to koza.ai@proton.me. Requests are resolved no later than 30 (thirty) days from the date of application.

13. Data Accuracy and Liability for Health Decisions

The accuracy of health data entered into the app (feeding times, sleep durations, illness symptoms, temperature readings, etc.) is the user's responsibility.

Insights, suggestions, and EPDS feedback produced by the AI are for informational purposes only; they do not constitute medical diagnosis, treatment, or medication recommendations. For any decisions regarding your baby's or your own health, always consult a pediatrician, gynecologist, or relevant healthcare professional.

Kılınç Labs cannot be held responsible for the consequences of health decisions made based on AI outputs produced from incorrectly or incompletely entered data.

14. Crisis Situations

If a high-risk level is detected in the postnatal depression screening test (EPDS), the app will direct you to the following resources. This is not medical advice; it is provided to encourage you to seek professional support:

  • Alo SABİM (182): Ministry of Health Contact Center (Turkey) — free health counseling
  • Emergency (112): In case of life-threatening situations or suicidal thoughts
  • Turkish Psychiatric Association: For specialist psychiatric referral

15. Registration and Account Security

The user is obliged to provide accurate, complete, and up-to-date registration information. The confidentiality of account login information (Apple ID, Google account) is entirely the user's responsibility. If you detect unauthorized access to your account, report it immediately to koza.ai@proton.me.

16. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Kılınç Labs reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Important changes are announced via in-app notification or registered email address. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
